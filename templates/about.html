🌱 About AgroGuide

Smart Farming Solution for Better Crop & Fertilizer Decisions

📌 What is AgroGuide?

AgroGuide is an intelligent farming assistant that helps farmers select the best crops based on temperature, rainfall, soil type, and season. It also recommends the optimal fertilizers for each crop to maximize yield and maintain soil health.

🚀 Features

Smart Crop Recommendation

Get best crop suggestions instantly

Weather Based

Uses temperature & rainfall data

Fertilizer Recommendation

Suggests optimal fertilizers for crops

Soil Analysis

Supports multiple soil types

History Tracking

View past crop & fertilizer recommendations

🌾 Our Mission

To empower farmers with smart technology for better crop selection and fertilizer management, leading to higher productivity and sustainable agriculture practices.
